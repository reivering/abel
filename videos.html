Instructional Videos

1. Understanding Limits

Description: An intuitive introduction to limits, exploring what happens when a function approaches a specific value.

Learning Objectives:

  • Define a limit informally.
  • Evaluate one-sided and two-sided limits graphically.
  • Understand when a limit does not exist.

Key Concepts: \( \lim_{x \to c} f(x) = L \), Asymptotes, Continuity.

2. Introduction to Derivatives

Description: Discover the derivative as the instantaneous rate of change and the slope of a curve's tangent line.

Learning Objectives:

  • Compute average vs. instantaneous rate of change.
  • Understand the limit definition of the derivative.

Key Concepts: \( f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h} \), Tangents, Slope.
