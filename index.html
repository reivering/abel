What is Calculus?

Calculus is the mathematical study of continuous change. It provides a framework for modeling systems in which there is change, and a way to deduce the predictions of such models.

🎯

Limits

Understanding how a function behaves as it approaches a certain value. \( \lim_{x \to a} f(x) \)

📈

Derivatives

Measuring the instantaneous rate of change or the slope of the tangent line. \( \frac{dy}{dx} \)

📊

Integrals

Calculating the true area under a curve and accumulating quantities over time. \( \int_a^b f(x) \,dx \)

🌍

Real World Applications

Applying calculus concepts to solve dynamic real-life problems.

Why Calculus Matters

Physics

Kinetics, forces, and motion of objects.

Economics

Marginal cost, profit maximization, rates of change.

Engineering

System analysis, structural integrity, signals.

Machine Learning

Gradient descent, optimization of algorithms.
